Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change how often I receive promises?

Go to the Schedule tab. You can choose "Fixed Times" for morning, noon, and evening delivery, or switch to "Repeat Interval" and set any frequency from every minute to every 4 hours.

I'm not receiving notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled: go to the Schedule tab and tap "Enable Notifications." If that doesn't work, check your iPhone Settings > His Love > Notifications and ensure they're turned on. Also make sure Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode isn't blocking them.

How do I switch between KJV and NIV?

Go to the Schedule tab. At the top you'll see "Bible Translation" where you can pick King James Version (486 promises) or New International Version (258 promises).

How do I create and share a collage?

Tap the Collage tab. Choose a layout, pick photos from your library, select a Bible promise, and customize the text. Then tap "Save" to save to your camera roll or "Share" to post directly to social media.

My favorites disappeared after an update

Favorites are stored locally on your device. If you reinstall the app, local data may be cleared. We recommend screenshotting or sharing your favorite verses to keep them safe.

Does the app use any of my personal data?

No. His Love collects zero personal data. Everything is stored locally on your device. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
