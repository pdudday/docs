♥ His Love - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

His Love ("the App") is designed to deliver God's promises from the Bible to your device. We deeply respect your privacy and have built the App with that principle at its core.

Data Collection

His Love does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically, the App does not:

• Collect your name, email, or any personal information
• Track your location
• Use analytics or tracking tools
• Send any data to external servers
• Use cookies or similar technologies
• Contain any third-party advertising SDKs

Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (favorite verses, notification schedule, and translation choice) locally on your device using iOS built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Photos

The App requests access to your photo library solely for the collage creation feature. Photos you select are processed entirely on your device. They are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to deliver Bible promises at your chosen schedule. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No push notification servers are involved.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page with an updated date. Since the App collects no data, we do not anticipate any material changes.
